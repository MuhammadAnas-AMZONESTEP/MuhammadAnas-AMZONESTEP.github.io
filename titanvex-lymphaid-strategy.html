02 — Diagnosis

Root Cause Analysis

  • 01
    Campaign Structure Inefficiencies
    Multiple match types coexist within the same campaigns alongside excessive keyword quantities — in several cases between 50 and 100 per campaign. This prevents Amazon's algorithm from identifying winning search terms and distributes budget inefficiently, creating slower learning cycles and inconsistent performance.
  • 02
    Lack of Negative Targeting
    Broad and Auto campaigns operate without sufficient negative keyword implementation. This creates significant budget leakage by continuously funding low-relevance search terms that are unlikely to convert, constraining overall account efficiency.
  • 03
    Overexposure to Competitive Keywords
    Historical campaigns are heavily focused on broad category terms — Lymphatic Drainage Supplement, Lymph Cleanse, Detox Supplement — that carry high search volume but also elevated CPCs. For a product with limited review authority and a 34% gross margin, this creates unnecessary profitability pressure.
  • 04
    Limited Social Proof
    A review count of 28 and a 4.1-star rating place TitanVex Lymphaid at a significant conversion disadvantage relative to more established competitors. This impacts both advertising performance and organic ranking potential.
  • 05
    Declining Organic Visibility
    Reduced sales velocity has contributed to weakened keyword ranking. As organic visibility diminishes, dependence on paid traffic intensifies — creating a reinforcing cycle that becomes increasingly difficult and costly to sustain.

06 — Retention & Authority

Supporting Growth Initiatives

Subscribe & Save Growth

Recurring customers represent the highest-value asset in supplement retail. The current 13-subscriber base provides a foundation; an aggressive first-order incentive will accelerate enrollment and improve revenue predictability.

  • Introduce 20% first-order coupon alongside existing 10% S&S discount — creating a perceived 30% saving
  • Objective is customer lifetime value, not single-order conversion
  • As subscription volume grows, advertising dependency decreases
  • Revenue becomes more predictable and structurally stronger

Review Acquisition Strategy

Increasing review authority is a parallel business objective that directly influences conversion rate, advertising efficiency, organic ranking, and customer trust.

  • Request a Review automation for all fulfilled orders
  • Amazon Vine participation to build initial review volume
  • Enhanced customer support touchpoints
  • Improved packaging experience to drive organic advocacy
  • Follow-up sequence optimization
