02 · The Strategy

Three full-funnel pillars

Growth doesn't come from one tactic — it comes from balancing acquisition, catalog, and efficiency so they reinforce each other. Each pillar has a clear goal, the moves behind it, and the outcome it drives.

Pillar 01

Acquire

Bring new customers into the brand — the engine that makes growth compound.

  • New-to-brand Sponsored Brands & video
  • Top-of-funnel keyword & audience discovery
  • Competitor-conquesting placements
  • Promotions to seed first purchases
OutcomeA growing base of new customers and a wider top of funnel to convert.
Pillar 02

Activate the Catalog

Turn the expanded, in-stock range into ranked, converting products.

  • Structured launch plan per new SKU
  • Listing & A+ optimization tied to what converts
  • Variation & bundle strategy
  • Protect in-stock continuity on hero SKUs
OutcomeMore products earning rank and sales, not just the original best-sellers.
Pillar 03

Compound Efficiency

Convert early investment into organic strength and tighter advertising returns.

  • Harvest converting search terms; cut waste
  • Protect & strengthen stable organic rank
  • Bid & budget discipline via Scale Insights
  • Balance paid and organic sales mix
OutcomeImproving ROAS and ACOS as the account matures — efficiency that lasts.
03 · The Recommendations

The specific moves we recommend — and why

These are the priority actions for the first two quarters. Each is paired with the reasoning so the logic is clear, and the result we expect it to produce.

01

Lead with new-to-brand acquisition

Sundae Body's growth compounds fastest when new customers keep entering the funnel. We'll prioritize new-to-brand campaigns and conquesting before chasing pure efficiency, because a wider customer base lifts every downstream metric.

A larger, repeatable customer base that feeds organic rank and repeat sales.
02

Give every new SKU a real launch

An expanded catalog only pays off if each item gets ranked. We'll run structured launches — targeted keywords, honeymoon-period bids, and review velocity — so new products become winners instead of stalling.

Newly added items contributing meaningful revenue within their first 60–90 days.
03

Optimize listings around what actually converts

Conversion rate is the multiplier on every ad dollar. We'll tune titles, bullets, images and A+ using converting search terms and review insights, so traffic turns into orders at a higher rate.

Conversion held in the 10–15% range, improving the efficiency of all spend.
04

Mine search terms continuously, cut waste weekly

Spend leaks through non-converting queries. Using Scale Insights automation plus AI-assisted search-term analysis, we harvest winners into exact match and negate the waste — every week, with full context.

Lower wasted spend and a steady path toward the ACOS target.
05

Defend and sharpen an already-strong organic position

Sundae Body's organic ranks are already stable — a real asset. Rather than rebuild, we'll protect those positions and push them further, converting paid momentum on priority keywords into even stronger organic placement so the brand carries more of each sale.

An even healthier paid-to-organic mix and improving long-term profitability.
06

Plan ahead of the seasonal peak

The account's strongest demand lands in Q4. We'll use predictive forecasting for spend and inventory so we scale into the peak prepared — not reacting once it's already here.

Maximum seasonal revenue capture without stockouts dragging on ads.
