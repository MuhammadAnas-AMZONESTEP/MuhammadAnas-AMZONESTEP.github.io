⬡ Executive Note · Inventory

Inventory availability remains one of the most significant factors affecting Amazon performance. Our strategy focuses on maximizing efficiency and growth opportunities for available foam products while maintaining consistent stock on primary SKUs — and preparing campaign and keyword structures so future product lines can be onboarded smoothly as they become available.
